About Us

The Knights of Iluvatar are a noble kinship in the world of Lord of the Rings Online. We are dedicated to upholding the values of honor, courage, and fellowship as we adventure through the lands of Middle-earth. Our kinship welcomes both seasoned veterans and newcomers alike, united in our love for Tolkien's rich lore and the camaraderie of fellow adventurers.

Founded in the early days of LOTRO, our kinship has grown into a tight-knit community of players who support each other in all aspects of the game. From epic raids to casual strolls through the Shire, we believe in creating memorable experiences for all our members.

Why Join Us?

Community

Be part of a friendly and supportive kinship that values teamwork and mutual respect. Our members come from all walks of life, united by their passion for Middle-earth.

Adventures

Participate in regular group activities, from epic raids to casual explorations of Middle-earth. We organize weekly events catering to all levels and interests.

Growth

Learn from experienced players and grow your skills in all aspects of the game. Our mentorship program ensures that every member has the support they need to thrive.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements to join the Knights of Iluvatar?
We welcome players of all levels and experience. The main requirements are a positive attitude, willingness to participate in kinship activities, and respect for fellow members.
How often does the kinship organize events?
We have scheduled events several times a week, including raids, group quests, and social gatherings. There are also impromptu events organized by members regularly.
Is there a required time commitment?
We understand that real life comes first. While we encourage regular participation, there's no strict time commitment. We value quality of interaction over quantity.
