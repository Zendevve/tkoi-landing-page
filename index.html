Our Fellowship

Forged in the fires of adventure, the Knights of Iluvatar stand as guardians of Middle-earth. Our kinship is more than just a group of players; we are a fellowship bound by honor, courage, and friendship.

From the lush fields of the Shire to the depths of Moria, we face the shadows that threaten our realm. Our ranks welcome both seasoned veterans and bright-eyed newcomers alike.

Join us and etch your name in the annals of Middle-earth's history!
