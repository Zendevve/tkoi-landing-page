🏰 About Us

Knights of Iluvatar is a friendly and active kinship dedicated to exploring Middle-earth together. Whether you're a seasoned veteran or new to LOTRO, you'll find a home with us.

🎯 Activities

Join us for regular raids, instances, festivals, and social events. We organize weekly group activities and help members with their personal quests and achievements.

👥 Community

Our kinship values respect, cooperation, and friendship. We maintain an active Discord server and regular in-game meetings to keep our community strong.

🌟 Benefits

Enjoy shared resources, experienced mentors, and a helpful community. We provide support for crafting, gear acquisition, and character development.
